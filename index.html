1. Request Your Data from Instagram

  • Use the direct link below to jump straight to Meta's Download Center.
  • Choose "Create Export" > "Export to Device".
  • Customize Information to only "Followers and Following".
  • When all these steps are done, it should match the screenshot.
JUMP TO DOWNLOAD CENTER

Pro Tip: Make sure to set the Format to JSON and the Date Range to "All Time"!

2. Upload Your .zip File

  • Once your file arrives via email, download it and upload it above.
  • This normally takes Instagram a few minutes, but in rare cases may take multiple days.
  • Your data is processed entirely in your browser memory, so we can't see anything!

3. Clean the List

  • Click a username to verify their profile.
  • Hit Dismiss to move them to the Graveyard once you unfollow that traitor, or if they are a celebrity (they not following u back lmaooo).
  • Click Restore if you want to move someone back to the Hit List.
