1. Request Your Data from Instagram

Open Instagram and navigate to Settings > Accounts Center > Your Information > Download Your Information. Request a download, select "Some of your information", and check "Followers and Following". Ensure the format is set to JSON.

[Image: Instagram Export Interface Screenshot]

2. Upload Your .zip File

Once your file arrives via email (usually within 5-10 mins), download it and upload it above. Note: Your data is processed entirely in your browser memory. Nothing is ever uploaded to a server.

3. Clean the List

Click a username to verify their profile. If it's a deleted account or a celebrity, hit Dismiss to move them to the Graveyard. If you make a mistake, you can Restore them anytime.
